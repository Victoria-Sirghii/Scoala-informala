TEA SHOP

TEA SHOP has been supplying its speciality teas in Europe 5 years. Operating from its warehouses in the Switzerland, TEA SHOP is a family owned business with community ties in the Southern Asia Area.

TEA SHOP sources its speciality teas from farms all over the world (see tea origins). The assortments are freshly packed in special bags, with a triple layer of protection, which maintain the quality of the plants and the special scents.

We welcome you to explore our specialty tea range.
